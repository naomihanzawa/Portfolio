GRAPHIC DESIGN

ALL EYES ON US

There were two kinds of exhibitions, about photography called "through the lens" and A.I(Artificial Intelligence) called "All Watched Over" at this exhibition called"All eyes on us" altogether. I designed two posters.
First idea, I made camera’s lens with eyelash so that express all eyes(cameras) on us in A.I era.
Second idea, I used the picture which is one of the work in this exhibition.
And expressed also the title "all eyes( I expressed with macbook’s window in this time) on us".
